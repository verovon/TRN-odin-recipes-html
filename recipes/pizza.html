Pizza recipe

The topping for these vegan pizza is ULTRA flavorful, satisfying and easy to make. The pizza can be made on the oven or using a microwave. No one will miss the meat and dairy!

This pizza recipe happens to be vegan, but you don’t have to be vegan to love it. It’s easy to make, it’s flavorful and fresh, and you can even meal prep it if you want to get ahead on lunch or dinner.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomatoes
  • Vegan cheese
  • Aubergine
  • Onions

Steps

  1. First, add the tomatoes and cheese
  2. Cut the onions and the aubergines
  3. Add the rest of the ingredients.
