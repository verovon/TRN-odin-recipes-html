Burrito recipe

The filling for these vegan burritos is ULTRA flavorful, satisfying and easy to make. The mixture can be made on the stove top or using an Instant Pot. No one will miss the meat and dairy!

This burrito recipe happens to be vegan, but you don’t have to be vegan to love it. It’s easy to make, it’s flavorful and fresh, and you can even meal prep it if you want to get ahead on lunch or dinner.

Ingredients

  • Tortillas
  • Tempeh
  • Tahini
  • Lettuce
  • Avocado

Steps

  1. First, prep the fillings.
  2. cook the Spanish rice.
  3. make a quick guacamole with mashed avocado, garlic, lime juice, and salt
  4. Next, fill the burritos.
  5. Finally, fold them closed.
